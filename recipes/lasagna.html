Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me a lot of time. This dish satisfies everybody in the family. Try it tonight!! Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn't have to be a tedious job. This top rated lasagna recipe comes together quickly with relatively short ingredient list.


Ingredients

  • Turkey: This easy lasagna starts with ground turkey
  • Spaghetti Sauce: Use store-bought or homemade spaghetti sauce
  • Eggs: Eggs help bind the cheese mixture together. Plus, they provide moisture and richness
  • Seasonings: Season the easy lasagna with dried parsly, salt and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles: Ofcourse, you'd need lasagna noodles
  • Water: Pour 1/2 cup of water around the edges of the baking dish before baking

Steps to make

  1. Cook and drain ground turkey, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and the seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe
  4. Bake, covered for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 45 minutes
