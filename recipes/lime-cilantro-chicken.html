Lime Cilantro Chicken


Homepage

Description

This slow cooker cilantro lime chicken is bursting with flavour. Try it in tacos for a nice change from plain chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package dry taco seasoning mix
  • 1 medium lime, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 3 pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2
    Stir salsa, taco seasoning, lime juice and cilantro together in a slow cooker until well combined. Add chicken and spoon salsa mixture over top to coat

  3. Step 3
    Cover and cook untill chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, on Low for 6-8 hours or High for 4 hours. An instant read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degree F (74 degrees C)

  4. Step 4
    Shred chicken in the crock with two forks, then mix with the sauce

  5. Step 5
    Enjoy!!
